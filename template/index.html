Rain in Australia

Feature list in order to use THRIVE's Predictor

  • Location - The common name of the location of the weather station
  • Minimum Temperature - The minimum temperature in degrees celsius
  • Maximum Temperature - The maximum temperature in degrees celsius
  • Rainfall - The amount of rainfall recorded for the day in mm
  • Evaporation -The so-called Class A pan evaporation (mm) in the 24 hours to 9am
  • Sunshine - The number of hours of bright sunshine in the day.
  • Wind Gust Direction - The direction of the strongest wind gust in the 24 hours to midnight
  • Wind Gust Speed - The speed (km/h) of the strongest wind gust in the 24 hours to midnight
  • Wind Direction at 9 am - Direction of the wind at 9am
  • Wind Direction at 3 pm - Direction of the wind at 3pm
  • Wind Speed at 9 am - Wind speed (km/hr) averaged over 10 minutes prior to 9am
  • Wind Speed at 3 pm - Wind speed (km/hr) averaged over 10 minutes prior to 3pm
  • Humidity at 3 pm - Humidity (percent) at 3pm
  • Pressure at 9 am - Atmospheric pressure (hpa) reduced to mean sea level at 9am
  • Pressure at 3 pm - Atmospheric pressure (hpa) reduced to mean sea level at 3pm
  • Cloud at 9 am - Fraction of sky obscured by cloud at 9am. This is measured in "oktas", which are a unit of eigths. It records how many
  • Cloud at 3 pm - Fraction of sky obscured by cloud (in "oktas": eighths) at 3pm. See Cload9am for a description of the values
  • RainToday - Boolean: 1 if precipitation (mm) in the 24 hours to 9am exceeds 1mm, otherwise 0
